== Description==
Create your own free booking system using the booking commerce WordPress booking plugin. You can easily embed Book Now button with your Wordpress website and start accepting bookings in few minutes. Your customers can easily create bookings and you can manage them as per your convenience.

To get the detailed view of how to create free booking system using booking commerce visit [Booking Commerce User Guide](https://user-guide.bookingcommerce.com/).

Add-Ons like Google Calendar and PayPal helps in better calendar management and accepting payments online via Credit/Debit card to smooth line the overall booking process.

What's more, you can do both Single and Multi-User bookings. Add multiple agents and provide them limited admin access. Have total control on customer reviews and get your business growing forward.

== Features ==

[Manage Booking](https://www.bookingcommerce.com/en/features/manage-bookings/):

Easily manage all your customer booking according to your schedule and needs.Addition to that, let your customer free and create bookings on behalf of them.

[Unlimited Paid Booking Creation](https://www.bookingcommerce.com/en/features/create-unlimited-booking-products/):

Create as many paid bookings as you want on the go.Easily create booking with complete details regarding location, availability and custom fields.

[Unlimited Free booking Creation](https://www.bookingcommerce.com/en/features/free-bookings/):

In Addition to that, you can also create unlimited bookings for the free services and products.

[Multi-Users Access](https://www.bookingcommerce.com/en/features/multi-users/):

Create multiple agents and assign privilege to control your admin panel.

[E-mail Templates](https://www.bookingcommerce.com/en/features/email-templates/):

Create automated customised e-mail templates for enthralling customer experience.Not just you can create customised templates, but also you can add small snippets of code in the form of placeholders anywhere inside the content.

[Booking Reschedule](https://www.bookingcommerce.com/en/features/reschedule-bookings/):

Have more flexibility in customer bookings by scheduling and rescheduling bookings as per yours and customers convenience.

[Customer Reviews Management](https://www.bookingcommerce.com/en/features/customer-reviews-management/):

Manage reviews given by your customer and filter the most relevant reviews to be shown.

[App Integration](https://www.bookingcommerce.com/en/addons/):

For better customer experience, you can integrate multiple apps like Google Calendar, Cash on Venue, PayPal and ICS File with bookingcommerce.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-booking-commerce` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Configure the plugin by going to Settings -> Booking.
4. Place `<?php echo Booking_Commerce::script();?>` in your templates
5. Enter the Booking Commerce domain and Save Changes.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does Booking Commerce integrate with any other apps? =

The service integrates with PayPal and Google Calendar.

= What platforms does Booking Commerce support? =

This is a SaaS application that is accessible by any internet connected device.

= Does Booking Commerce offer multi-user capability (e.g. teams)? =

Yes, there is both single user and multi-user booking.

= Who are the main user groups of Booking Commerce? =

User groups include people who provide a service on an hourly basis. Usually appointment booking for doctors, lawyers, freelancers, consultants, spa owners, and etc.

= Does Booking Commerce offer guides, tutorials and or customer support? =

Yes, the service provides a knowledge base for all your common queries and a dedicated customer support.
